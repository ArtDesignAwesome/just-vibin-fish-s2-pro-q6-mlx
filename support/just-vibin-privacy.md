# Just Vibin' Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 22, 2026

Just Vibin' is designed as a local-first AI voice scene studio for iPhone.

## What the app stores locally

Just Vibin' stores your scripts, voice samples, voice references, generated audio, diagnostics, and model readiness state inside the app container on your device. These files are used to provide local voice generation, voice reference preparation, render history, and troubleshooting diagnostics.

## Network use

The app uses internet access for first-time model downloads, model verification or repair, App Store purchases, and advertising. The app does not require hosted voice synthesis for generation after a selected local engine is installed and ready.

## Microphone and speech recognition

If you choose to record or transcribe a voice reference, the app may request microphone and speech recognition permissions. These flows are user controlled and are used to prepare voices for local generation.

## Advertising and purchases

Just Vibin' is free with ads and offers a one-time Remove Ads Forever purchase. The app uses Google Mobile Ads for banner/interstitial advertising. Google Mobile Ads may collect or process data described by Google's advertising SDK disclosures, including identifiers, approximate location inferred from network information, usage/ad interaction data, diagnostics, and performance data depending on SDK behavior and user settings.

## Data sharing

Just Vibin' does not sell your scripts, recordings, or generated audio. App-owned voice and render files are intended to remain on device unless you choose to export or share them.
