# Just Vibin' Support

Just Vibin' is a local AI voice scene studio for iPhone.

## Getting Help

For setup, model downloads, voice recording, rendering, purchases, ads, or bug reports, use the public support tracker:

https://github.com/ArtDesignAwesome/just-vibin-fish-s2-pro-q6-mlx/issues

When reporting a problem, include your iPhone model, iOS version, selected engine, and any copyable diagnostic report from the app settings.

## Common Notes

- First setup downloads large model files. Keep the app open on Wi-Fi until the selected voice engine says it is ready.
- Voice generation runs locally on device after the selected engine is installed.
- Ads should never block local rendering. If an ad is unavailable or the device is offline, the app should continue using its in-app support/removal card.
- Use only voices you own, have permission to use, or are otherwise allowed to use.
